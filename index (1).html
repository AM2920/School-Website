St. Xavier's School

Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

The Church was originally built from 1896-1897, but it burned down and was not rebuilt until 1977 and opened the following year. The Church was the seed from which this school grew. It was the original building and this shcool actually used to be a church, but that changed in 1980 with the construction of additional buildings, such as the school library.



School Library

Was built in 1980

Built in 1980, this was the first educational building on the school campus and was originally a private library reserved for church members, but it was made the school library in 1986 with the further expansion of the school.



School auditorium

Was built in 1982

The School auditorium was originally built in 1979, but it was redesigned and rennovated with better seating and technology in 1982, and it was rennovated again between 2005 and 2007, then again in 2018.
